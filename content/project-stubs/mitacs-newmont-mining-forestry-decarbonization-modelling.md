# Mitacs, Newmont, And Forestry Decarbonization Modelling

Status: Past project

## Public Summary

A Mitacs Accelerate industrial postdoc project with Newmont on
forest-management scenarios as nature-based decarbonization options for the
mining sector.

The project connected mining-sector net-zero planning, voluntary carbon-market
quality concerns, forest carbon modelling, and ecosystem-service indicators in
a prototype decision-support framework. The public page should keep the framing
at the research-program level and avoid exposing private project files,
site-specific working data, internal review history, or partner-confidential
material.
