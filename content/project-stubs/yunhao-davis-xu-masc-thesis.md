# Yunhao (Davis) Xu MASc Thesis

Status: Ongoing project

## Public Summary

Yunhao (Davis) Xu's MASc thesis is working-titled _Modeling Salvage
Harvesting After Wildfires: A Linear Bi-level Principal-agent Approach_.

The project develops decision-support models for post-wildfire salvage
harvesting in British Columbia. It frames the planning problem as a
principal-agent relationship between the forest landowner or policy-maker
and forest companies, then uses rolling-horizon and optimization methods to
test salvage schedules and policy instruments under changing wildfire
conditions.
