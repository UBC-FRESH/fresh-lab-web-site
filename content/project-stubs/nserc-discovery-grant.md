# NSERC Discovery Grant

Status: Ongoing project

## Public Summary

The FRESH Discovery Grant research program develops open, scalable, and
reusable modelling tools for integrated sustainable forest management and
forest-sector supply-chain capacity planning.

NSERC Discovery Grants provide base funding for a PI research program. This
page should therefore describe the grant as the FRESH lab's base-funding
platform behind many connected modelling, software, thesis, and partnership
projects rather than as a single stand-alone study.
