# Jamie Iversen MASc Thesis

Status: Ongoing project

## Public Summary

Jamie Iversen's MASc thesis is working-titled _A Fuel Treatment Planning
Decision Support Tool_.

The project develops modelling workflows for landscape-level wildfire
fuel-treatment planning in British Columbia. It focuses on how forest
managers can choose the timing, placement, and intensity of fuel treatments
across large areas while accounting for treatment cost, wildfire risk, forest
structure, and budget constraints.
