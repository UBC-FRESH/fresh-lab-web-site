# CCCANDiES

Status: Ongoing project

## Public Summary

CCCANDiES stands for _Cumulative effects of Climate Change, Anthropogenic
and Natural Disturbances on forest Ecosystem Services_.

The project develops open cumulative-effects modelling infrastructure for
forecasting how managed forest landscapes respond to climate change, wildfire
and other natural disturbances, timber harvesting, road networks, and
forest-sector supply-chain decisions.

The technical core is interoperability: connecting ecosystem-process
simulation tools such as SpaDES, LandR, fire modules, and carbon modules with
forest estate and wood-supply optimization tools such as ws3. The goal is to
represent stochastic ecosystem processes and explicit management decisions in
the same reproducible workflow.
